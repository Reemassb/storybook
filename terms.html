What you are buying

A licence code that lets you create one personalized digital storybook using our web app, and download it as a PDF. This is a digital product — nothing is shipped.

How the code works

  • Each code carries a fixed number of image credits (16), which cover the full book plus a few spare images for redoing pages.
  • For The Magic Passport, one code creates one country's book. Once the first image is generated, the code is fixed to that country.
  • Codes are for personal, family use.

Your photo and your book

You confirm you have the right to upload the photo you use. The book you create is yours to keep, print and give as a gift. Please do not resell the generated books or the licence codes.

Availability

The app depends on third-party services (Cloudflare, OpenAI, Gumroad). Occasional interruptions can happen. If a technical fault on our side stops you finishing your book, contact us and we will fix it or restore your credits.

Illustrations

Illustrations are generated by AI from your photo. Results vary; the likeness is a stylized cartoon interpretation, not a photograph. Spare credits are included so you can regenerate pages you are not happy with.
