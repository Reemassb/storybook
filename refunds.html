Refunds & Delivery

Last updated: 24 July 2026

Delivery

Delivery is instant and by email. After purchase, Gumroad emails you a licence code. You enter that code in our web app, choose your options, upload a photo, and your book is created in a few minutes. You then download it as a PDF. Nothing is posted; there are no shipping costs.

Price

The price is shown on the product page before you pay, in US dollars, processed by Gumroad.

Refunds

  • If your code has not been used to generate any images, you may request a full refund within 14 days of purchase.
  • Once images have been generated, the product has been delivered and consumed, so refunds are not normally possible — real generation costs are incurred for each image.
  • If something goes wrong technically — the app fails, credits are lost, or your book cannot be completed — contact us. We will restore your credits or refund you.

Problems with your book

If a page does not look right, use the spare credits included with your code to regenerate it. If you have run out and are still not happy, email us and we will usually add credits so you can finish.

How to reach us

Email reemafahmi@gmail.com or reply to your purchase email. We answer within 2 business days.
